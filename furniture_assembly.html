Assembly FAQs

Do I need to carry the boxes up to the room?

Not at all! Just let us know where the boxes are when we arrive, and our team will move them to the final room before we begin assembly.

Will you haul away the cardboard boxes?

We will break down all cardboard and organize the trash neatly for your recycling bin or garage. If you need us to physically haul it off the property to the dump, we can do so for a small extra disposal fee.

Talk to the owner.

Send us your list, and the owner will reach out directly with a flat-rate price. No dispatch fees or corporate runaround.
