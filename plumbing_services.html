Plumbing FAQs

Do you provide the parts?

We carry standard repair parts (flappers, basic piping, wax rings). If you are upgrading a specific fixture (like a new vanity faucet), we ask that you purchase the unit you like, and we will handle the installation.

Are you licensed plumbers?

We are professional, insured handymen who specialize in standard residential plumbing repairs and swaps. If your project requires a master plumber (like moving main sewer lines), we will let you know upfront!

Send us your list, and the owner will reach out directly with a flat-rate price. No dispatch fees or corporate runaround.
